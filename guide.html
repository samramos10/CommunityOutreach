Here's Your Guide

Building up the courage to help others does not need to be in a series of steps. Any way you find most comfortable is fine. Although, here you will find a sort of guide to help you and lead you in the right path. If you decide to use other methods, then that's completely fine. Being independent from the start is also very useful.


Try Your Hardest To Be Your Best Self

Many times, oneself cannot help others without helping themselves first. So, it's important that you take care of your well-being in order to be there for others in your communities.


Have Set Goals

If you don't already, try doing small random acts of kindness. Small acts of kindess help you figure out what exact change you'd like to see in the groups surrounding you.


Problem-Solve Situations Small Or Big

Everyone can have some conlict at some point in their lives ranging from small to big. It's important that we find resolutions not only to benefit ourselves, but to benefit others as well. Finding a solution to conlflicts can make each and every one of us more knowledgable in any situation that may come our way in the future.


Stay Up To Date

Stay up to date with any new events that are soon to come. In times like this, staying up to date with health guidelines and new information regarding COVID-19 is useful as you can spread this information. Find stay-at-home volunteer services for your safety and the others around you.


Do Your Research

Once you've set your goal, it's may be useful to search for local organizations that you'd like to volunteer in that peak your interest. Research this certain organization or program to see what volunteer oportunities are available for you. Find contact information in case any questions arise or to reach out and lend a helping hand.
