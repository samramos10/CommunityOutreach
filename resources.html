Your Resources

Here are some websites and other useful tools that you can use. Feel free to share on the public commenting post any links or tips that you know of and use this to make connections!


Websites

Teen Life provides you with nationwide opportunities for community service as a high school student. Just choose your location and find what you can do.

College Vine gives you information about extracurricular activities and what kind of safe services you can partake in even during COVID-19.

Volunteer Match allows you to filter and personalize volunteer opportunities for a specified cause and location.
